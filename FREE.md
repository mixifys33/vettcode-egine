## FAQ

### Q: Is there a catch?

**A:** No catch! It's genuinely free. We built this to help developers, not to make money.

### Q: Will you start charging later?

**A:** No plans to charge. It's open source, so even if we wanted to, you could fork it and keep using it for free.

### Q: How do you afford to run this?

**A:** Users deploy their own instances on free tiers (Vercel, etc.). We don't host a central service, so we have no hosting costs.

### Q: What if I exceed Vercel's free tier?

**A:** Vercel's free tier is very generous. If you exceed it, you can:

- Deploy on another free service
- Self-host on your own server
- Upgrade Vercel (optional, ~$20/month for heavy usage)

### Q: Do I need to pay for OpenRouter?

**A:** No! OpenRouter has free models that work great. Paid models are optional if you want faster/better results.

### Q: Can I use this for my company?

**A:** Yes! MIT License allows commercial use. Deploy your own instance and use it freely.

### Q: Will my code be sent to your servers?

**A:** No! When you deploy your own instance, your code stays on your Vercel deployment. We never see your code.

### Q: Can I remove the "VettCode Engine" branding?

**A:** Yes! MIT License allows you to modify and rebrand as you wish.

### Q: Can I contribute to the project?

**A:** Yes! Contributions are welcome. It's open source on GitHub.
